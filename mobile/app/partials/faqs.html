Frequently Answered Questions

 

How do I login to MYRV?

Click on the MYRV logo on the top left of the desktop webpage to get to the MYRV home page. On the left side you should see a blue button that says Login (or Logoff). Click on the Button and you will be taken to the Login page. For mobile devices the Login (or Logoff) Button is displayed prominently at the bottom of the page. Once you are logged in you should see your Avatar on the top left of the Desktop navigation menu or at the very bottom of the mobile version.

How do I capture a Gas Fill, Special Event, Overnight Stay, Restaurant, Friend, Service or Accident?

On the Desktop webpage click on the Capture selection. Once clicked it will display a list of capture-able events. On the mobile version click on the large Capture button and a list of capture-able events will be displayed. All captured events are linked to Trips.

Once initiated, details for the selected capture-able event will be displayed. Simply enter and Save

How do I Manage a Gas Fill, Special Event, Overnight Stay, Restaurant, Friend, Service or Accident after it has been Captured?

On the Desktop webpage click on the Manage selection. Once clicked it will display a list of manageable events. On the mobile version click on the large Manage button and a list of manageable events will be displayed. All managed events are linked to Trips.

Once a manageable event is selected, a list of those event details will be displayed associated with a selectable trip. To manage a detail from one of the list items - click on the item in the list and details associated with that selection will be displayed.

From here you can Update, Add or Delete a detail. When adding a new detail be sure to hit the Clear button first.

How do I View a Gas Fill, Special Event, Overnight Stay, Restaurant, Friend, Service or Accident after it has been Captured?

On the Desktop webpage click on the View selection. Once clicked it will display a list of viewable events. On the mobile version click on the large View button and a list of viewable events will be displayed. All viewed events are linked to Trips.

Once a viewable event is selected, a list of those event details will be displayed associated with a selectable trip. To view a detail from one of the list items - click on the item in the list and a detail page will be displayed.

There is no update capability with View.

Can I export data for my own use?

You can export any of the Capture-able details to a csv file which can be read and managed by Excel or Macos Numbers. On the Desktop webpage click on the View selection. Once clicked it will display a list of viewable events. On the mobile version click on the large View button and a list of viewable events will be displayed.

Once a viewable event is selected, a list of those event details will be displayed associated with a selectable trip. To export trip detail for the selected details - click on the export button.

What is the Current Snapshot?

All information is Trip-centric. When you set a trip as active (only one trip can be active), it will come up as default trip selection especially for Capture selections.

The Current Snapshot shows information from your active trip.

I want to change my Avatar. How can I do that?

Send us an image that you want to replace your avatar with. We will size it to fit into our scheme and let you know when it is active.

How can I change my information like my password, address, bio etc?

After you are logged in select your avatar at the top right of the desktop web page. On the mobile version the Avatar is at the bottom of the page. Clicking on this will bring you to a page where you can update your personal information.

How can I use my phone or other mobile device to use MYRV?

We have a mobile version of MYRV. You can get to it by clicking myrv.xyz

How can I contact you if I have any questions?

You can reach me by clicking on the Feedback icon at the bottom of the either the desktop or mobile version. There is also a email link on the bottom of the desktop version.
